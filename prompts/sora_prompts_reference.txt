# Reference Prompts for Various Scenes

“Aerial Arrakis Panorama”:  
- Panoramic view of endless dunes at dusk, ultra high-definition.

“Fremen Hideout”:  
- Interior cave with water collectors, earthy tones, torchlight.

“Bene Gesserit Reverie”:  
- Close-up of a Bene Gesserit Sister’s face under a hood, intense eyes.

“Sandworm Emergence”:  
- Massive worm breaching, sand scattering in slow motion.

Use these as references or starting points. Contributors can blend these prompts or tweak details.
